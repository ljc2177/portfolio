I am a computational designer, fundraiser, and musician based out of Brooklyn, NY. I am currently pursuing my Master's in Computational Design Practices at Columbia University GSAPP. In all of my work, I love to tell the stories we most need to hear by using interactive visualizations. In the past, I have worked in fundraising for the Museum of Food and Drink, WYEP & WESA, and the Pittsburgh Zoo & PPG Aquarium.

When I'm not working, I'm traveling with my partner and our two dogs on a quest to find delicious food.
